Water Sports

At Pine's Resort you get the chance to learn some of the most fun water activities. Or if you are already familiar, you can also rent the needed equipments. Water sports such as jetskiing, windsurfing, water-skiing and much more are available at Sun Island Resort & Spa.

Diving

Undoubtedly, Baga Beach is the most happening beach of Goa. Full of travellers, adventure sports, and everything that you can ask for in a Goa’s beach. There are few spots around this beach which are really enjoyable for scuba diving. The beach is especially good for first time divers. This is one of the safest places in Goa to try diving and exploring the underwater world

About Us

Situated in the peaceful surroundings of Colva Beach, this stunning resort is one of the most popular in the Gao. The resort is easy to access, taking about 17 minutes from Panji International Airport. This gives guests a chance to see firsthand the amazing array of beach in the area. Each room is beautifully designed and offers air conditioning, a television with satellite channels, and a balcony area to relax and enjoy the marvelous setting. Pine's Resort & Spa houses several top class restaurants including Sun Star which specializes in fresh seafood and a wonderful Italian restaurant which is perfectly located on the Jetty and makes an ideal spot for newlyweds or couples celebrating their wedding anniversary.
